Eagles are one of the biggest birds on the planet.
They are at the top of the food chain.
Their eyesight is around 5 times better than the vision of a human.
There are more than 60 eagle species.
Eagles can see up to 3 kilometers away.
Eagles build their nests on high cliffs or in tall trees.
Bald eagles are not actually bald. 
Female golden eagles usually lay between one and four eggs each breeding season.

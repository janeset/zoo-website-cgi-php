These birds are super swimmers and impressive divers. 
They can reach depths of over 500m and stay underwater for up to 22 minutes!
Emperor penguins are the largest of all the different kinds of penguin.
On average they measure 115cm tall, that's about the height of an average six year old.
Emperor penguins spend their entire lives in Antarctica.
In Antartica temperatures can drop to as low as -60 degrees celcius!!
